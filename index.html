A Few Words About the C.V.Raman Global University

C.V. Raman Global University offers two BSc programs at the undergraduate level. The BSc programme at CGU is open to aspiring candidates, and the fee ranges from INR 3.3 LPA to INR 5.6 LPA. However, the fee structure may vary based on the programme of study and other factors.

The BSc programme at C.V. Raman Global University offers two specialisations, one of which is Agriculture, a captivating field of study. The university provides full-time, four-year undergraduate BSc (Hons.) Agriculture.
